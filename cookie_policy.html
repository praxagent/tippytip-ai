Privacy & Storage Policy

Last Updated: July 15, 2024

This Privacy & Storage Policy explains how TippyTip ("we", "us", or "our") prioritizes your privacy by avoiding cookies and using minimal local storage on our website. This policy is designed to help you understand what data we store locally, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your data.

1. Our Cookie-Free Approach

We are committed to respecting your privacy. Unlike many websites, we do not use cookies of any kind - not even "essential" cookies. Instead, we use browser localStorage for the minimal functionality needed to remember your consent preferences.

2. What is localStorage?

localStorage is a web storage method that allows websites to store data directly in your browser. Unlike cookies:

  • localStorage data is never sent to our servers
  • localStorage data is only accessible from the same domain that created it
  • localStorage data persists even after you close your browser
  • localStorage data doesn't have an expiration date (but can be cleared by you at any time)

3. How We Use localStorage

We use localStorage for only one purpose:

Storage Key Purpose Data Stored
tippytip_cookie_consent Remembers that you've seen and accepted our consent banner The value "essential" (a simple text string)

4. No Third-Party Tracking

We do not use any third-party tracking or analytics services on our website. This means:

  • No analytics services like Google Analytics
  • No social media tracking
  • No advertising or marketing trackers

5. How to Clear localStorage

If you wish to clear the localStorage data from our website, you can do so through your browser settings:

Chrome

  1. Click the three dots in the top right corner
  2. Select "More tools" > "Clear browsing data"
  3. Select "Cookies and other site data" and "Cached images and files"
  4. Click "Clear data"

Firefox

  1. Click the three lines in the top right corner
  2. Select "Options" > "Privacy & Security"
  3. Under "Cookies and Site Data," click "Clear Data"

Safari

  1. Click "Safari" in the top menu
  2. Select "Preferences" > "Privacy"
  3. Click "Manage Website Data"
  4. Search for "tippytip.ai" and click "Remove"

6. Changes to Our Policy

We may update our Privacy & Storage Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top.
